About Mahajan Hospitals

Founded by Dr. Kondal Rao Annimalla, we are dedicated to providing exceptional medical care to the Vijayawada community and beyond.

Mahajan Hospitals is a leading healthcare institution in Vijayawada, committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centric medical services. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern technology and staffed by a team of experienced and compassionate healthcare professionals.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive and affordable healthcare for everyone. We believe in a holistic approach to healing, focusing on not just treating illness but also promoting wellness and a healthy lifestyle for all our patients.
